The Parenting Paradox

13 Dec 2020

The purpose of parenting is teaching the kids how to become successful. Paradoxically, success cannot be taught.

It's not difficult to teach success. It's impossible.

  • By their own estimate, most parents aren't successful
  • Successful parents don't understand what made them successful
  • Whatever made them successful, does not apply today
  • Whatever made them successful, is not what their kids need to become successful
  • What even is success?

The Solution

The gate to heaven is sealed in paradox. The key, in this case, as in many others, lies in our mental models.

What is teaching? Is it transmitting knowledge, or creating an enviroment for learning.

Ge barnen kärlek, mera kärlek och ännu mera kärlek, så kommer folkvettet av sig själv.
